**📥 HOW TO IMPORT YOUR OPTIMIZED CSV**

**SHOPIFY — Step by Step:**

1. Log into your Shopify admin
2. Click **Products** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Import** (top right, next to Export)
4. Click **Add file** and select the CSV I've attached
5. ✅ Check the box: **"Overwrite existing products that have the same handle"**
   *(This is critical — it ensures your existing listings get updated, not duplicated)*
6. Click **Upload and continue**
7. Review the preview — confirm product count matches your catalog
8. Click **Import products**
9. Shopify will notify you by email when the import is complete

**⚠️ SHOPIFY IMPORT TIP:** If you have more than 50 products, the import may take a few minutes. You'll get a confirmation email from Shopify when it's done.

---

**WOOCOMMERCE — Step by Step:**

1. Log into your WordPress admin
2. Go to **WooCommerce → Products**
3. Click **Import** at the top of the page
4. Click **Choose file** and select the attached CSV
5. Click **Continue**
6. On the column mapping screen, confirm fields are mapped correctly
7. ✅ Check **"Update existing products"**
8. Click **Run the importer**
9. Wait for the completion screen — then check a few products to confirm updates

---

**BIGCOMMERCE — Step by Step:**

1. Log into your BigCommerce control panel
2. Go to **Products → Import**
3. Click **Choose file** and upload the attached CSV
4. Select **Overwrite existing items based on SKU**
5. Click **Import**
6. BigCommerce will show a progress bar — wait for completion
7. Spot-check 3–5 products to confirm the optimization applied correctly

**WHAT TO EXPECT NEXT**

Google typically re-crawls and re-indexes updated pages within **1–4 weeks**. You won't see search ranking changes overnight — but once Google picks up the optimized metadata, your listings will be eligible to rank for the keywords we've targeted.

For best results:
- Don't change the optimized titles or meta descriptions for at least 30 days
- Add product images if any listings are missing them (images + alt text = stronger SEO signal)
- Consider running Google Shopping ads on your top 10 products while organic rankings build
